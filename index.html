Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

How do I download wallpapers?
To download a wallpaper, simply browse our collection, find a wallpaper you like, and click the "Download" button. The wallpaper will be saved to your device in its original high-resolution format.
Are all wallpapers free to download?
Yes, all wallpapers on CheDex Wallpapers are completely free to download and use for personal purposes.
What resolution are the wallpapers?
Our wallpapers come in various resolutions, with many available in 4K (3840x2160) or higher. The specific resolution is listed on each wallpaper's details.
Can I submit my own wallpapers?
We're not currently accepting public submissions, but we're working on adding this feature in the future. Stay tuned!
How often do you add new wallpapers?
We add new wallpapers to our collection on a weekly basis, so be sure to check back regularly for new content.
Can I use these wallpapers for commercial purposes?
The wallpapers on CheDex Wallpapers are intended for personal use only. For commercial licensing inquiries, please contact us through our Contact page.
How do I set a downloaded wallpaper as my background?

The process varies depending on your device:

- Windows: Right-click the image file and select "Set as desktop background"

- Mac: Right-click the image and select "Set Desktop Picture"

- iOS: Open the image in Photos, tap the share button, and select "Use as Wallpaper"

- Android: Open the image, tap the menu button, and select "Set as wallpaper"
